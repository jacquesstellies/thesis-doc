\section{Nadafi Controller}
In \cite{nadafiRobustBacksteppingAttitude2021} Nadafi and Kabganian present a backstepping controller for attitude control of an underactuated satellite. Their contribution is the design of a novel hypersurface to ensure stability and convergence to the desired attitude. This hypersurface is defined as:
\begin{equation}
    -2\begin{bmatrix}
        aq_{e2} q_{e3} + q_{e0} q_{e1} \gamma_1 \ b q_{e1} q_{e3} + q_{e0} q_{e2} \gamma_2
    \end{bmatrix}^T
\end{equation}
